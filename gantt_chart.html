📝 Implementation Notes & Dependencies

Critical Success Factors

Early buy-in from volunteer teachers, robust IT support, and consistent coaching presence throughout the pilot period.

Key Milestones

Teacher Onboarding (Sept 16), Pilot Launch (Sept 29), Mid-Point Review (Oct 20), Final Assessment (Nov 15).

Risk Mitigation

Built-in buffer time, backup teacher recruitment, and alternative timeline if technical issues arise.

Resource Allocation

2 FTE coach hours/week, IT support on-call, administrative oversight bi-weekly, teacher stipends for participation.
