Curipod Implementation Brief

AI-Powered Learning Platform for WCSD

Executive Summary

Curipod offers WCSD a strategic opportunity to implement AI-powered educational technology that aligns with our 2022-2027 Strategic Plan priorities of student engagement, literacy advancement, and DEI initiatives. This brief outlines implementation benefits, stakeholder value, and a phased rollout approach for Fall 2025.

Strategic Alignment with WCSD Goals

Student Voice & Engagement: Curipod's 100% participation tools ensure every student contributes to classroom discussions through AI-powered interactive elements.

Literacy Focus: Platform provides real-time writing feedback and rubric-aligned assessment tools that support our district-wide literacy initiatives.

DEI Integration: Built-in translation capabilities and scaffolded language supports advance our commitment to equity and inclusion.

Device Compatibility: Curipod works seamlessly with our existing 1:1 Chromebook initiative and WITS system integration.

Investment & ROI Analysis

Cost Structure: District licensing model provides predictable per-student pricing with volume discounts for WCSD's 10,000 student enrollment.

Time Savings: Conservative estimates show 3-5 hours per week of teacher planning time recovery, translating to increased instructional focus and reduced overtime costs.

Academic Impact: Early adopter districts report 20-46% improvement in student writing outcomes and engagement metrics.

Recommended Next Steps

Approve pilot program with 3-5 volunteer teachers across elementary, middle, and high school levels. Establish success metrics aligned with WCSD Strategic Plan goals. Prepare for potential district-wide scaling based on pilot outcomes and stakeholder feedback.
